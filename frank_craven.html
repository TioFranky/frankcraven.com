Frank Craven is a regular columnist for the STREET NEWS newspaper, and a contributor to various other magazines and papers. He also produces a weekly TV news program since 1999 on MNN Public Access (In Manhattan) that's called:
What's Ailing/Healing USAmerica?
Webstreamed via www.mnn.org, or Channel 34, 9:30 pm Friday nights in Manhattan

He's also a regular poet on the downtown scene and has performed in various venues with performance art, and has staged more than 10 original Off-Off-Broadway productions, many viewable at the New York Public Library for the Performing Arts at Lincoln Center Theater On Tape archive. (AEA) plays.
